In Search of Wyland Walls

Sometimes you find a wall where you don’t expect to see one.  We went to Minneapolis to visit family last week.  When I think of Minnesota, oceans and whales are not the first thing that comes to mind but I figured it would not hurt to check the list of walls and sure enough there was one at the Zoo. It is always much harder to research a wall that is not out in public so you’re never really sure if it is going to be there but we were able to find Our Ocean Family and were surprised by how good a shape it is in.  Even though it’s been there for 16 years it was in great shape.  It was a great addition to our trip.
